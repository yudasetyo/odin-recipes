Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a baked pasta dish that originated in Italy in the Middle Ages. It's made by layering wide, flat pasta sheets with sauces, cheeses, and other fillings, then baking and slicing into squares.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Meat
  • Ricotta
  • Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
