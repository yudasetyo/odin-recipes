Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is a pasta dish that's typically made with eggs, cheese, cured pork, pasta, salt, and black pepper. It's a staple of Italian cuisine and is especially associated with Rome, though some say it originated in the Lazio region.

Ingredients

  • Pancetta
  • Pecorino Cheese
  • Parmesan
  • Eggs
  • Spaghetti
  • Garlic Clove
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Sea Salt and Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook pasta
  2. cook bacon
  3. Whisk eggs, cheese, and half and half
  4. Drain pasta
  5. Add pasta to egg mixture
  6. Add bacon, toss, and serve
